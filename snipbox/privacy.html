SnipBox – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2 November 2025

SnipBox (“the Extension”) is a Chrome browser extension developed by Jakša Tomović. We respect your privacy and believe your data should stay on your device.

1. Data Collection

SnipBox does not collect or store any personal data.

  • No names or email addresses
  • No browsing history
  • No IP addresses or tracking analytics
  • No payment information

2. Local Storage Only

All saved snippets and settings remain locally on your device using Chrome's storage.local API. We do not transmit or access this data.

3. Snippet Saving

SnipBox only reads selected text when you manually choose “Save to SnipBox.” Nothing is scraped or monitored in the background.

4. Licensing & Payments (DodoPayments)

When purchasing a license, payments are processed securely by DodoPayments.

  • SnipBox does not receive or store your financial data.
  • Customer information is handled according to DodoPayments’ policies.
  • The extension only verifies whether a license key is valid.

5. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer data to third parties. We do not use data for advertising or analytics.

6. Exports

If you export your snippets (e.g. JSON or Markdown), the file is created locally. No upload happens.

7. Changes

If SnipBox introduces cloud sync or new online features in the future, this policy will be updated in advance.
